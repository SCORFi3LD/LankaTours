River cruises and boat tours in Sri Lanka

The rivers in Sri Lanka have partly dimensions of European lakes, a boat trip is therefore appropriate. On the Madhuganga, near the town of Balapitiya on the southwest coast, there is also plenty to see: birds, monitor lizards, crocodiles.

On a small island in the middle of the river lives a family, which shows how a household in Sri Lanka once worked. Here palm mats are braided, cinnamon peeled, ropes turned and stories told.

Also on the reservoirs in the interior, you can make boat tours. How about a catamaran trip on the lake of Giritale? Since I'm close by, I can offer you a special program here: we go fishing and then have a BBQ on the shore.

I also enjoy a boat trip on the lagoon of Batticaloa. There are lots of jellyfish and eager fishermen who are smiling friendly in your camera. These tours will take you to the center of the city, to the gate of Batticaloa.
