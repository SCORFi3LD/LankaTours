Gems of Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka is famous for its precious and semi-precious stones since biblical times - are mainly sapphires, but also pink rubies, blue moonstones, tourmalines, grenades, spinels, aquamarines, blue topazes, cat's eyes, quartz.

At present, only in and around the city of Pelmadulla is being mined, the photos above are from there. The nearest town is Rathnapura, which is surrounded by agricultural land in the "nowhere" and is considered the capital of gems.

Here, as a visitor, you can follow live how the gems are mined, even today using ancient methods. In wood-supported shafts, the miners work under almost inhuman conditions; wages are only available if successful.

You are also happy to show you how the gems are washed out. Hard to believe that these precious stones are in a lump of soil. A raw gemstone can only be discovered by a professional, for an untrained eye he remains invisible.
