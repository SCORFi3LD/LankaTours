A day at Giritale Lake

At my hometown on the lake of Giritale

Should you visit with me the old royal capital Polonnaruwa or stay overnight in the area, I like to show you the environment in which I grew up or where my family lives.

I would like to invite you for a catamaran cruise on the lake of Giritale. We hope to catch a few fish so we can have a little barbecue on the shore. Fresh sea fish, roasted on an open fire, is also a specialty here.

If you like it sporty, you can also play a game of cricket with my friends and me. Cricket is the national sport in Sri Lanka, similar to football. Here each child knows the rules and is the owner of a cricket bat.

And if you like, you can end the evening with one or two glasses of Arrak. Arrak is our high-proof national drink, obtained from the flowering of the coconut palm.
