Sigiriya - The Lion Rock of Sri Lanka

Here is probably the highlight of your tour! Even from a distance, you can see the 200-meter-high monolithic rock of Sigiriya and let anticipation arise. Already the walk through the front garden is nice, always with a view of the rocks.

Halfway up are the cloud girls. Ornate frescoes, about 1500 years old. Great, richly decorated décolletés, tropical flowers, elaborate hairstyles, graceful movements - the age you really do not look at these girls.

Continue along the mirror wall to the lion's paws. Here begins the climb. Those who are not free from giddiness should probably forego the way the path is exposed and the spartan fuse.

At the top there is a Sri Lanka at your feet. Kassipa I (477-495 AC) had withdrawn here after he had put his brother and rightful heir to the throne to flight.
