Colombo - the capital of Sri Lanka

I am happy to show you our capital Colombo. Foremost for me is the Galle Face Green, the former horse race of the colonial rulers. Today, couples and tourists stroll here in the sunset.

Colombo also has a number of temples to offer. The biggest is the Gangaramaya with its great prayer hall on Baira Lake, a classic Bawa building. An excellent and quiet place in the middle of the lively capital.

The Pettha is the old commercial district between the harbor and the train station. Here is still like a hundred years ago negotiated and negotiated. A walk through the narrow streets is a great experience and gives insights into Colombo's original business world.

Colombo also has a number of great buildings to offer as a photo opportunity: the City Hall on the American model, the Independence Hall, the National Museum - or even the Twintowers of the World Trade Center in the 1st district.
