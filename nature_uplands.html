The Uplands of Sri Lanka

Here we are at the center of tea cultivation, home of the world-famous Ceylon tea. I will gladly show you how the tea grows, is picked and processed. A guided tour in the Blue Field tea factory will bring you closer to the topic.

You are also welcome to take a ride on the old railway through the tea plantations or the highlands, an incredible experience. The starting point of your choice, I'll pick you up at the destination station.

Nuwara Eliya, the city above the clouds, was already popular with the colonialists due to the moderate temperatures. Today it is a small town with a golf course, racecourse, an artificial lake, and chic English-style cottages.

Other attractions include the small botanical garden of Hakgala, interesting temples and the wooden bridge at Badulla. Various waterfalls, which can be reached after short hikes, invite you to a refreshing bath.
