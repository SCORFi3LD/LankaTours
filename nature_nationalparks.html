National Parks in Sri Lanka

In Sri Lanka, there are a variety of national parks, the main attraction is mostly large elephant herds.

  • Bundala National Park in Southern Province: Bird Park
  • Gal Oya in the Eastern Province: Boat safaris, especially in winter to see many migratory birds
  • Horton Plains in the highlands: Ideal for long hikes
  • Hurulu Eco Park, Minneriya and Kaudala National Park: Theses three parks in the area of ​​Habarana and Polonnaruwa - the animals can walk between the three parks.
  • Pigeon Island in Eastern Province: Known for its pigeon population
  • Udawalawe in Uva province: Big elephant herds
  • Wilpattu in the northwestern province: Famous for its leopards
  • Yala National Park in the south: Probably the best known among all the national parks of Sri Lanka
