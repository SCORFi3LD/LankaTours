Hiking & mountaineering in Sri Lanka

The most famous mountain in Sri Lanka is probably the Adams Peak with a height of over 2200 meters. It is a pilgrimage destination of all religions and is usually climbed at night, as the sunrise is sensational.

A great hike is also the "Worlds End" in the Horton Plains National Park. Here the mountain drops steeply over 1000 m. On a clear day, in the morning, you can even see to the sea - later, fog is usually the result.

The Pidurutalagala with its 2534 m is the highest mountain of the island of Sri Lanka and may now be climbed again. For many years he was locked because there is the largest transmitter on the summit.

Beautiful hikes can also be made in the Nuckles Mountains near Kandy and south of the highlands near Haputale.
