Elephant Rides

The elephant has been a workhorse in Sri Lanka since ancient times. The mahouts, the elephant guides, spend an entire lifetime working with an animal to pull wood or move other heavy goods.

Today you can take advantage of such a tame elephant and ride on it. Silently, the giant animal walks through jungles and rivers, you just have to sit on your back and enjoy the scenery.

Especially great is a ride near the Sigiriya Rock. Here are beautiful photo opportunities with the monolith in the background. The mahout will most likely make a few tricks for the elephant.

Also on different beaches of the southwest coast elephant riding is offered. Wedding and honeymoon couples should ride on an elephant because it promises happiness.
